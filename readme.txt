== Description ==

Kar Dealer is a multilingual and multicurrency theme for dealership to manage, list and sell your new or used cars, motorbikes, motorcycles and vehicles online. Usefull also for Trucks, Scooters, Side-By-Side, ATV, Utilitie ATV, Campers,  RVs, Motorhome, Travel Trailer, Trailers,  Lifts, Trator, Forklift & Lift Truck, Snowmobile, MotoSnow, Snow Motorcycle, Motorbike Motor Full, Agriculture Equipment  and even Aircrafts,  Jets Aircrafts,  Helicopters,  Turboprops,   Charter,  Airplanes,  Aerotrader AirPlanes and much moreï¿½with our free MultiDealer plugin.Exclusive configurabile free plugin with Advanced search for cars, range slider for price, and 2 templates (list view, gallery). Responsive, Social menu available, wooCommerce compatible and full color control. 3 footer widgets, Layout Full or boxed, sticky header and Back to top Button. Choose a Light or Dark skin with one click. Hacker protection plugin install with just one click. No coding required. With StartUp guide, guide online, support, faq page, demo and 3 min demo video (www.KarDealerTheme.com)

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.

2. Type in car dealer in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.

3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.

4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
